Introduction

You wake up in an unknown room. There is a door, and a computer terminal next to it. Unfamiliar metal tiles are on the floor, walls and ceiling. You don't remember how you got there nor who you are. The holographic computer screen shows a date in it's top right corner. It is the year 2096. Find out what happened by solving puzzles.

Gameplay

To advance to new rooms and areas, you will need to hack the terminals and write code to bypass the security system, find access codes and keys to open locked doors throughout the map. There are various items scattered around the map that you can use to your advantage. Special skills and weapons can also be unlocked, which will become especially important later.
